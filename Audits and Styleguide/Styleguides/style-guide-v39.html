Page Zone System

Four tonal bands from top to bottom. The gradient header and dusty rose-grey footer bookend the page; the warm neutral intro band and near-white content zone provide visual rhythm between them.

Zone 1 — Header
Gradient #ffffff→#fafafa · paper grain texture · 3px deep-teal top stripe · nav is sticky white below
Zone 2 — Intro Band
Warm neutral #e9e8e6 · white intro card (two-layer shadow) + stats bar (white, teal #CCDCE0 border) float on it
Zone 3 — Content / Cards
Near-white #fafafa · white cards sit on it with standard shadow
Zone 4 — Footer
Dusty rose-grey #D2CECA · bookend echoes header tone, closes page symmetrically

About This Site

Why This Site Exists

When my child was diagnosed with SETD5 Syndrome, I found myself in a difficult position. It was a rare diagnosis, and there weren't many accessible resources out there to help me understand what it meant or where to start. I spent a lot of time searching, reading research papers written for geneticists, and trying to piece together information from different sources scattered across the internet.

I realized that other families would face the same challenge. So I created this site—a place where families navigating a SETD5 Syndrome diagnosis could find clear, straightforward information without having to sift through medical jargon or spend hours hunting for resources.

What You'll Find Here

This site includes guides for newly diagnosed families, summaries of published research written in plain language, a medical terms glossary, and practical tools like disability discounts and school IEP guidance. Everything is organized to be easy to navigate and understand, whether you're a parent, educator, medical professional, or someone else in the SETD5 community.

How This Was Made

While this site was created by one parent, it was built with input and support from others in the SETD5 community. Some content was developed with the help of AI tools to ensure accuracy and clarity, but all information has been reviewed with family insights in mind. This is a resource created for families, by someone who understands what it's like to navigate a rare diagnosis.

Questions or Feedback?

If you have suggestions, corrections, or would like to share your family's story, please reach out. This site is meant to grow and improve based on what families in our community need most.

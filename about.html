About This Site

Dear Friend,

Why This Site Exists

When my child was diagnosed with SETD5 Syndrome, I found myself in a difficult position. We had been stuck in what is known as a diagnostic odyssey for several years before we finally got our diagnosis. It was a rare diagnosis, and there weren't many accessible resources out there to help me understand what it meant or where to start. I spent a lot of time searching, reading research papers written in technical language, and trying to piece together information from different sources scattered across the internet.

I knew that if I was struggling to find answers, you probably would too. So I built this site—a place where you can find clear, straightforward information about SETD5 Syndrome without having to sift through medical jargon or spend hours hunting for resources.

What You'll Find Here

I've put together guides for newly diagnosed families, summaries of published research written in plain language, a medical terms glossary, and practical tools like disability discounts and school IEP guidance. I've organized everything to be easy to navigate and understand, whether you're a parent, educator, medical professional, or someone else in the SETD5 Syndrome community.

How This Was Made

I built this site on my own, but with support from others in the SETD5 Syndrome community. I understand what it's like to navigate a rare diagnosis, and I made this for you—because no family should have to start from scratch the way I did.

Questions or Feedback?

If you have suggestions, corrections, or would like to share your family's story, please reach out. I want this site to grow and improve based on what families in our community need most.

Sincerely,

Kate

Made with ♥ by a parent of a child with SETD5 Syndrome, for families like ours.

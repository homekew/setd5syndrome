Design Principles

Warm & Approachable

The palette combines warm brown with vibrant blue-grey to create an approachable, family-focused aesthetic that feels distinct from institutional or corporate designs.

Clear Hierarchy

Color is used intentionally to guide the eye: charcoal for primary content, blue-grey for emphasis and structure, and warm brown for actions and navigation.

Accessibility

All color combinations maintain sufficient contrast for readability. The vibrant blue-grey (#4f8f9e) has strong contrast against both the cream background and charcoal text.

Consistency

Every element uses only the five approved colors. This creates a cohesive, unified visual system across all pages.

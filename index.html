This website was created by a parent of a child with SETD5 Syndrome to help other families better understand the condition. The information shared here is based on published research and insights from the SETD5 community and is intended for educational purposes only.

A note for international readers: Most medical and research content on this site applies globally. Some sections cover US-specific programs and laws, so please check your country for equivalent support systems. A Google Translate widget (powered by Google) is available in the top corner of most pages.

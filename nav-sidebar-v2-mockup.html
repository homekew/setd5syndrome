Pros

  • Header is the very first thing — page identity is immediate
  • Feels more like a magazine or editorial site
  • Nav feels like it "belongs to" the content, not floating above everything
  • Same scroll behavior as Option A — nav always accessible

Cons

  • Nav is not visible until you scroll slightly on very tall headers
  • Slightly more complex CSS (nav needs position:sticky;top:0 while being below header)

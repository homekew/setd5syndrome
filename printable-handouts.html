Handout Builders & Downloads

Free one-page guides designed to download, print, and bring to appointments.

Each handout fits on a single printed page. They're designed for families to hand to providers, school staff, or anyone who is new to SETD5 Syndrome, without requiring them to read a lengthy document first. These resources are for educational purposes only and are not intended to replace the guidance of a qualified medical, educational, or therapeutic professional.

Palette Assessment

Distinctness from SETD5 Syndrome Foundation: The Realtime Colors palette is visually distinct from the Foundation's teal + coral + gold combination. Where the Foundation uses bright, energetic tones, this palette is earthy and grounded—charcoal, cream, warm tan, and sage create a completely different visual language that feels more intimate and family-focused rather than institutional.

Warmth & Approachability: The warm tan (#565246) and sage (#a7c4ba) together create a naturally warm aesthetic that supports the "built by a parent, for families" positioning. The charcoal feels trustworthy without being cold, and the cream background maintains the approachability you want.

Functional Use: Charcoal for primary text and navigation, cream for backgrounds, warm tan for primary accents (buttons, highlights), sage for secondary callouts and supporting elements, and muted purple-grey for tertiary actions. This creates clear visual hierarchy without overwhelming.

Note on Tan Warmth: The warm tan is slightly more muted than the current Honey Gold. If you want additional visual pop, consider warming it slightly to #a78a5f or #a89566 while maintaining the earthiness.
